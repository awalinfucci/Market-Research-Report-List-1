<p><ul><li>Structure-based Drug Design (SBDD)</li><li>Ligand-based Drug Design (LBDD)</li><li>Sequence-based Approaches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The computational drug discovery market is segmented into three primary types: Structure-based Drug Design (SBDD), Ligand-based Drug Design (LBDD), and Sequence-based Approaches. SBDD utilizes the three-dimensional structure of biological targets to design molecules, enhancing specificity and efficacy. LBDD relies on the knowledge of existing ligands to identify new candidates based on chemical similarity and binding affinity. Sequence-based approaches leverage genetic information to predict how proteins interact with potential drugs, guiding the discovery of suitable compounds for therapeutic use.</p></p>
