<p><p>The Semiconductor Green Laser market is primarily segmented into two types: QCW (Quasi-Continuous Wave) lasers and Continuous lasers. QCW lasers emit short bursts of high-intensity light, ideal for applications requiring precision and high energy in brief intervals, such as in medical and laser processing. In contrast, Continuous lasers provide a steady output of light, suitable for applications like displays and lighting where consistent brightness is crucial. Both types cater to diverse sectors, enhancing versatility in usage.</p></p>
