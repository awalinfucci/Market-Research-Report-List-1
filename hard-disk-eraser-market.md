<p><p>The hard disk eraser market includes three primary types of degaussers: coil degaussers, capacitive discharge degaussers, and permanent magnet degaussers. Coil degaussers generate strong magnetic fields through electrical coils, effectively erasing data from hard drives. Capacitive discharge degaussers utilize stored electrical energy to produce a high-intensity magnetic pulse for data destruction. Permanent magnet degaussers use strong permanent magnets to disrupt magnetic fields, ensuring data is irretrievable. Each type caters to different security needs and operational efficiencies in data sanitization.</p></p>
