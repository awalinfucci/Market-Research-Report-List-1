<p><p>The chemical trace analysis market encompasses various types, each focusing on specific aspects of material assessment. Contamination analysis detects and quantifies unwanted substances in samples, ensuring purity and safety. Chemical composition analysis identifies and characterizes the molecular makeup of materials, while elemental trace analysis examines the presence of trace elements in samples for quality control. Trace metals analysis specifically targets the detection of harmful metals within products or environments, crucial for compliance with safety regulations and environmental protection.</p></p>
