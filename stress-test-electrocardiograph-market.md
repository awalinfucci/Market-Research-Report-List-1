<p><p>A Stress Test Electrocardiograph, also known as an exercise ECG or treadmill test, is a diagnostic tool that evaluates cardiac function under physical stress. It measures the heart's electrical activity while the patient exercises, typically on a treadmill or stationary bike, to identify any abnormalities that may indicate underlying heart conditions. This form of testing is crucial for diagnosing coronary artery disease, assessing exercise tolerance, and guiding rehabilitation programs.</p><p>The Stress Test Electrocardiograph Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, increased awareness of heart health, and advancements in technology.
