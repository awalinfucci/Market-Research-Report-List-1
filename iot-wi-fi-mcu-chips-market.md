<p><p>The IoT Wi-Fi MCU chip market is categorized into single band and dual band types based on their frequency capabilities. Single band chips operate on one frequency, typically 2.4 GHz, offering a cost-effective solution for basic IoT applications. In contrast, dual band chips support both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, providing improved performance, reduced interference, and higher data rates. Dual band options are increasingly favored for more demanding applications, enhancing connectivity in diverse environments.</p></p>
