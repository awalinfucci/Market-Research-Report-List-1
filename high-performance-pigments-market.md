<p><p>The High Performance Pigments (HPP) market is divided into two main types: Organic HPP and Inorganic HPP. Organic HPPs are known for their high durability, vibrant colors, and excellent lightfastness, making them suitable for coatings, plastics, and inks. On the other hand, Inorganic HPPs offer enhanced stability, chemical resistance, and opacity, often used in applications like automotive finishes and construction materials. Both types cater to industries demanding superior quality and performance in color applications.</p></p>
