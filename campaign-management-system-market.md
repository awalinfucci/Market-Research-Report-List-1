<p><p>Campaign Management Systems (CMS) are categorized into two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise solutions are installed and operated on a company's local servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based CMS are hosted on remote servers and accessible via the internet, providing scalability, ease of use, and lower upfront costs. This flexibility allows businesses to quickly adapt to changing marketing needs and reduce infrastructure burdens.</p></p>
