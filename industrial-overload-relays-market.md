<p><strong>The Industrial Overload Relays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Overload Relays</li><li>Magnetic Overload Relays</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial overload relays market is primarily categorized into three types: Thermal overload relays, Magnetic overload relays, and Others. Thermal overload relays use a bimetallic strip that bends and triggers the circuit breaker when excessive heat is generated, providing reliable protection against overheating. Magnetic overload relays operate based on electromagnetic principles to detect overcurrent conditions and protect motors quickly. The "Others" category encompasses diverse technologies, including electronic overload relays, which offer advanced features such as adjustable settings and precise monitoring.</p></p>
