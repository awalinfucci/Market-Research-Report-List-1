<p><p>The automotive snow tire market primarily consists of three types: studded snow tires, studless snow tires, and others. Studded snow tires feature metal spikes for enhanced traction on icy surfaces, making them ideal for extreme winter conditions. Studless snow tires, on the other hand, use advanced rubber compounds and innovative tread designs to provide excellent grip on snow and wet roads without studs, suiting milder climates. The "others" category encompasses specialty tires, such as those designed for specific vehicles or unusual conditions.</p></p>
