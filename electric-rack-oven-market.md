<p><p>The electric rack oven market is segmented into single rack ovens and double rack ovens. Single rack ovens are designed for smaller operations, accommodating one rack of trays, making them ideal for businesses with limited space or lower production needs. In contrast, double rack ovens feature two racks, allowing for increased capacity and efficiency, suitable for medium to large-scale bakeries or foodservice operations. Both types offer precision cooking, consistent results, and energy efficiency tailored to various baking demands.</p></p>
