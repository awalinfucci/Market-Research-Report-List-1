<p><strong>The Railway Axle Counter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rail Side Axle Counter</li><li>On-rail Axle Counter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway axle counter market is primarily divided into two types: rail-side axle counters and on-rail axle counters. Rail-side axle counters are installed beside the tracks, utilizing sensors to detect the passage of train axles. In contrast, on-rail axle counters are mounted directly on the rail itself, offering a more compact solution. Both types are crucial for ensuring accurate train detection, improving safety, and optimizing railway traffic management by providing real-time data on train movements.</p></p>
