<p><p>The aquaculture fish feed market comprises three primary protein sources: insect protein, animal protein, and plant protein. Insect protein is derived from farming aquatic-friendly insects, offering a sustainable and nutritious option for fish feed. Animal protein, traditionally sourced from fishmeal or other animal by-products, provides essential nutrients but raises sustainability concerns. Plant protein, derived from crops like soy or peas, serves as an alternative that is often more environmentally friendly, though it may require additional supplementation to ensure balanced nutrition for fish.</p></p>
