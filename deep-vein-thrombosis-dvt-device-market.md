<p><p>The Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) device market includes various therapeutic options such as venous stents and intermittent pneumatic compression devices. Venous stents are used to maintain open blood flow in affected veins, reducing the risk of clot formation. Intermittent pneumatic compression devices promote circulation in the legs by applying rhythmic pressure, helping to prevent the development of DVT during long periods of immobility. Both types of devices play a crucial role in managing and preventing DVT effectively.</p></p>
