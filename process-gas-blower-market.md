<p><strong>The Process Gas Blower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement Blower</li><li>Centrifugal Blower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The process gas blower market is primarily categorized into positive displacement (PD) blowers and centrifugal blowers. Positive displacement blowers operate by trapping a fixed volume of gas and then displacing it to create pressure, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. In contrast, centrifugal blowers utilize rotary motion to accelerate gas through impellers, suitable for handling large volumes of air or gas at lower pressures. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, impacting efficiency and performance based on specific operational requirements.</p></p>
