<p><p>The pharmaceutical industry isolator system market consists of two main types: sterility test isolators and sterility production isolators. Sterility test isolators are designed for microbiological testing of sterile products, ensuring a contamination-free environment during testing procedures. On the other hand, sterility production isolators are utilized in the manufacturing process to maintain aseptic conditions while producing sterile pharmaceuticals. Both systems are essential for adhering to stringent regulatory standards and ensuring product safety in the pharmaceutical sector.</p></p>
