<p><p>The Far-field Speech Recognition market is categorized into three main types based on microphone configurations. Single microphones capture sound from a single direction, making them suitable for straightforward applications. Linear microphones consist of multiple microphones arranged in a line, enhancing directional sensitivity and noise reduction. Circular microphones feature an array positioned in a circular layout, allowing for comprehensive sound capture from all directions, ideal for environments with multiple speakers. Each type offers distinct advantages for varying speech recognition needs.</p></p>
