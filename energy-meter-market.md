<p><strong>The Energy Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromechanical Meters</li><li>Electronic Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The energy meter market is primarily divided into two types: electromechanical meters and electronic meters. Electromechanical meters, using mechanical components, measure energy consumption through rotating discs, making them simple and reliable but less accurate for advanced applications. In contrast, electronic meters utilize digital technology, offering precise measurements, data logging, and advanced features like remote monitoring, making them suitable for smart grid systems. The shift towards electronic meters reflects the increasing demand for efficiency and advanced energy management solutions.</p></p>
