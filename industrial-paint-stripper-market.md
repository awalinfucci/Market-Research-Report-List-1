Alkaline strippers utilize a basic pH to effectively break down paint and coatings, making them suitable for various surfaces. Acidic strippers, on the other hand, employ an acidic pH to dissolve paint, often used for tougher materials like metal. The "others" segment includes biodegradable and solvent-based options, catering to specific application needs. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the stripping requirements and surfaces involved.</p></p>
