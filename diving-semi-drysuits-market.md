<p><p>The diving semi-drysuits market is categorized into two primary types: hot water semi-drysuits and cold water semi-drysuits. Hot water semi-drysuits are designed for warmer aquatic environments, providing minimal insulation while allowing water circulation to regulate body temperature. In contrast, cold water semi-drysuits are engineered for colder conditions, offering enhanced thermal protection through thicker materials and sealed seams to minimize water ingress. Both types cater to specific diving needs, ensuring comfort and safety for divers across varying water temperatures.</p></p>
