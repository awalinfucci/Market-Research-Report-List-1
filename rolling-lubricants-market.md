<p><p>The rolling lubricants market is segmented into three main types: synthetic, semi-synthetic, and mineral-based lubricants. Synthetic rolling lubricants are engineered for high-performance applications, offering superior lubrication and thermal stability. Semi-synthetic rolling lubricants combine synthetic and mineral oils, providing a balance between performance and cost-effectiveness. Mineral-based rolling lubricants, derived from crude oil, are the most traditional option, valued for their affordability and availability. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on performance, environmental concerns, and economic factors.</p></p>
