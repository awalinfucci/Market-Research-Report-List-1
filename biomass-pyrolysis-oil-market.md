<p><p>Biomass pyrolysis oil market includes two primary types: fast pyrolysis and flash pyrolysis. Fast pyrolysis focuses on rapid heating of biomass in a controlled environment, maximizing liquid yield and producing bio-oil suitable for energy applications. In contrast, flash pyrolysis employs even quicker heating rates, leading to a more concentrated liquid product with improved chemical properties. Both methods aim to convert biomass into valuable liquid fuels, driving interest in sustainable energy solutions and reducing reliance on fossil fuels.</p></p>
