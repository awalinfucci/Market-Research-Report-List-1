<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Inkjet Heads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal MEMS Inkjet Heads</li><li>Piezo MEMS Inkjet Heads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet heads market primarily comprises two types: thermal MEMS inkjet heads and piezo MEMS inkjet heads. Thermal MEMS inkjet heads utilize heat to create vapor bubbles, forcing ink droplets through nozzles, making them ideal for photo and document printing due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, piezo MEMS inkjet heads employ piezoelectric elements to control droplet ejection, allowing for higher precision and versatility in printing on diverse surfaces, making them suitable for industrial applications and specialty inks.</p></p>
