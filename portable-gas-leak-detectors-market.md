<p><p>The portable gas leak detectors market comprises several types, each utilizing different sensing technologies. Electrochemical sensors measure gas concentrations through chemical reactions, while infrared sensors detect gases based on their absorption of infrared light. Semiconductor sensors rely on changes in electrical conductivity to identify gases. Catalytic sensors detect combustible gases by measuring heat produced during combustion. Other types may include photoionization and thermal conductivity sensors, providing diverse options for industries needing reliable gas leak detection solutions.</p></p>
