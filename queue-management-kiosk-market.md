<p><p>Queue Management Kiosks can be categorized into two market types: linear and non-linear. Linear markets involve a straightforward, sequential process where customers queue in a single line, receiving service in the order they arrive. In contrast, non-linear markets feature multiple service points and allow customers to choose their queue based on preference or urgency, leading to a more dynamic interaction. Each type is optimized for different environments, influencing customer flow and service efficiency in various settings.</p></p>
