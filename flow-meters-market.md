<p><p>The flow meters market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Electromagnetic Flowmeters** measure conductive liquids using magnetic fields. </p><p>2. **Vortex Flowmeters** utilize vortices created by fluid flow to gauge velocity. </p><p>3. **Coriolis Mass Flowmeters** assess mass flow through the Coriolis effect on vibrating tubes. </p><p>4. **Ultrasonic Flowmeters** employ sound waves to determine flow rate in various liquids or gases. </p><p>5. **Others** encompass devices like positive displacement and thermal flowmeters, offering diverse measurement solutions across industries.</p></p>
