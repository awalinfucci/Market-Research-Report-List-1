<p><p>The optical interconnect market encompasses various components and systems that facilitate high-speed data transmission. Cable assemblies provide the physical connections, while connectors enable easy integration. Silicon photonics systems leverage light for data transfer, and PIC-based interconnects utilize photonic integrated circuits for enhanced performance. Optical engines and transceivers convert electronic signals to optical ones. Free space optics transmit data through air, while fibers and waveguides guide light within materials, collectively enhancing bandwidth and reducing latency in communication networks.</p></p>
