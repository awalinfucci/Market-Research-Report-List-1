<p><p>The twisted pair cable market is primarily divided into two types: Unshielded Twisted Pair (UTP) and Shielded Twisted Pair (STP) cables. UTP cables are widely used for telecom and data networks due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. In contrast, STP cables include shielding to reduce electromagnetic interference, making them suitable for environments with high interference levels. Both types cater to different applications, from residential networking to industrial settings, balancing performance and protection needs.</p></p>
