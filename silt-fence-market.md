<p><p>The silt fence market is categorized into two main types: woven fibers and non-woven fibers. Woven fiber silt fences are made from interlaced materials, offering greater strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy soil and high-velocity conditions. In contrast, non-woven fibers are manufactured through a bonding process, resulting in lightweight and less expensive options, ideal for sediment control in less demanding settings. Both types effectively reduce soil erosion and improve water quality in construction and landscaping projects.</p></p>
