<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Handheld Laser Power Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Type</li><li>Thermoelectric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld laser power meter market comprises two primary types: photoelectric and thermoelectric. Photoelectric type meters utilize photodetectors to measure light intensity by converting laser energy into electrical signals, providing quick and precise readings. Thermoelectric type meters, on the other hand, gauge laser power by measuring temperature changes in a sensing element caused by absorbed laser energy. Both types serve industries requiring accurate laser measurements, such as manufacturing, telecommunications, and research, enhancing efficiency and safety in laser applications.</p></p>
