<p><p>The parenteral dosage form market includes various types, notably emulsions for injection, powders for injection, and gels for injection. Emulsions are liquid preparations that contain a mixture of oil and water, enhancing drug solubility and stability for intramuscular or intravenous delivery. Powders for injection are dry formulations that require reconstitution before administration, offering extended shelf life and customizable dosing. Gels for injection are semi-solid formulations that provide controlled release and enhanced bioavailability, suitable for targeted therapies and patient comfort.</p></p>
