<p><p>The transplant drug monitoring assay market is divided into two main types: chromatography combined with mass spectrometry (MS) and immunoassays. Chromatography-MS offers high sensitivity and specificity for quantifying therapeutic drug levels, making it ideal for detailed analysis. In contrast, immunoassays utilize antibodies for drug detection, providing faster results and ease of use, often preferred in clinical settings. Both methods are crucial for ensuring optimal dosage and minimizing adverse effects in transplant recipients, enhancing patient care and outcomes.</p></p>
