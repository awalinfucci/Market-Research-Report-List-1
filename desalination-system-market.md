<p><strong>The Desalination System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Desalination Processes</li><li>Multi-stage Flash Distillation (MSF)</li><li>Multiple Effect Distillation (MED)</li><li>Vapour Compression Distillation (VCD)</li><li>Membrane Desalination Processes</li><li>Electrodialysis (ED)/Electrodialysis reversal (EDR)</li><li>Reverse Osmosis (RO)</li><li>Ion exchange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desalination system market includes various technologies for converting seawater or brackish water into potable water. Thermal processes like Multi-Stage Flash Distillation (MSF) and Multiple Effect Distillation (MED) utilize heat to separate water from salts. Vapor Compression Distillation (VCD) employs mechanical energy for the same purpose. Membrane processes, such as Reverse Osmosis (RO) and Electrodialysis (ED/EDR), use selective membranes to separate ions. Ion exchange technologies facilitate the removal of specific ions, enhancing water quality for various applications.</p></p>
