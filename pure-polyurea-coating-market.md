<p><p>The Pure Polyurea Coating market is primarily categorized into two types: Aliphatic Polyurea Coating and Aromatic Polyurea Coating. Aliphatic polyurea coatings are known for their excellent UV resistance and color retention, making them ideal for outdoor applications. In contrast, aromatic polyurea coatings are cost-effective and offer good durability, but with limited UV stability, making them suitable for indoor applications or less exposed environments. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on performance characteristics and environmental exposure.</p></p>
