<p><p>Breathing disorders encompass a range of conditions affecting respiratory function. Asthma involves airway inflammation and constriction, often treated with inhalers and corticosteroids. Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) includes emphysema and chronic bronchitis, typically managed with bronchodilators and oxygen therapy. Allergic rhinitis causes inflammation due to allergens, treated with antihistamines and nasal sprays. Pulmonary hypertension leads to high blood pressure in the lungs, requiring medications and lifestyle changes. Cystic fibrosis impacts the lungs and digestive system, managed with enzymes and therapies, while idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis leads to lung scarring, treated with antifibrotic drugs and supportive care. Each market segment varies based on prevalence, treatment options, and ongoing research.</p></p>
