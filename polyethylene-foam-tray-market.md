<p><p>The Polyethylene Foam Tray market is categorized into two main types: Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) Foam Trays and High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) Foam Trays. LDPE Foam Trays are softer, flexible, and offer excellent cushioning, making them ideal for packaging delicate items. In contrast, HDPE Foam Trays are more rigid and durable, providing enhanced strength and resistance to wear, suitable for heavier products. Both types are widely used across various industries, including food packaging, electronics, and automotive sectors, due to their protective qualities.</p></p>
