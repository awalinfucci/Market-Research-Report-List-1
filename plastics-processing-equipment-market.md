<p><p>The plastics processing equipment market encompasses various techniques for shaping plastics. Injection molding involves injecting molten plastic into molds for precise shapes. Extrusion pushes heated plastic through a die to create long shapes like pipes and sheets. Blow molding forms hollow objects by inflating plastic within a mold. Thermoforming involves heating plastic sheets and shaping them over a mold. Additionally, other methods include rotational molding, compression molding, and 3D printing, catering to diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
