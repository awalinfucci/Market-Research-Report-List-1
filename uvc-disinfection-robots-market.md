<p><p>The UVC disinfection robots market comprises primarily two types: Ultraviolet Mercury Lamp Disinfection and UVC-LED Disinfection. Ultraviolet Mercury Lamp Disinfection utilizes traditional mercury vapor lamps, offering high-intensity UVC light for effective germicidal action, but with concerns over mercury toxicity and energy consumption. Conversely, UVC-LED Disinfection employs energy-efficient LED technology, providing targeted, low-wattage UVC output, while being more eco-friendly and having a longer lifespan. Both technologies are pivotal in enhancing hygiene in various environments, including healthcare, hospitality, and public spaces.</p></p>
