<p><p>Light curtains are safety devices used in industrial settings to create an invisible barrier that detects the presence of objects or personnel, enhancing safety around machinery. Primarily employed in automation, they prevent accidental access to hazardous areas during operational processes. Light curtains consist of a transmitter and receiver arranged in pairs, generating a grid of light beams. When an object interrupts these beams, the system triggers a response to halt machine operation, ensuring protection from potential injuries.</p><p>The Light Curtains Market is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing safety regulations and the adoption of automation in various sectors.
