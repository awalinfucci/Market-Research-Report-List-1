<p><p>The organic coconut sugar market is primarily divided into two types: powder and granular. Powdered coconut sugar is finely ground, making it ideal for baking and beverages, where quick dissolving is essential. It offers a smooth texture that blends seamlessly into recipes. Granular coconut sugar, on the other hand, retains a coarser texture, providing a more robust flavor and is often used as a direct sugar substitute in cooking and topping applications. Both types cater to diverse culinary preferences.</p></p>
