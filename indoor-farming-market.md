<p><p>Indoor farming encompasses various market types, including hydroponics, aeroponics, and others. Hydroponics involves growing plants in nutrient-rich water without soil, promoting faster growth and higher yields. Aeroponics, on the other hand, uses mist or spray to deliver nutrients directly to plant roots, requiring less water and eliminating soil-borne diseases. Other methods include aquaponics, combining fish farming with plant cultivation, and vertical farming, utilizing stacked layers to optimize space. Together, these innovative approaches enhance sustainable agriculture in urban settings.</p></p>
