<p><ul><li>Water-based Polyurethane Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-based Polyurethane Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EV polyurethane adhesives market is primarily divided into two categories: water-based polyurethane adhesives and solvent-based polyurethane adhesives. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly, offering low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and easy clean-up, making them suitable for various applications. Conversely, solvent-based adhesives provide strong bonding and durability, ideal for high-stress environments, but they typically contain higher VOC levels. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and furniture, catering to specific performance requirements and regulatory standards.</p></p>
