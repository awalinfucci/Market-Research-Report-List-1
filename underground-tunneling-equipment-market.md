<p><ul><li>Roadheader</li><li>TBMs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underground tunneling equipment market primarily encompasses Roadheaders and Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs). Roadheaders are versatile, continuous miners ideal for soft to medium-hard rock. They use a rotating cutter head to excavate tunnels, allowing for precise control and minimal vibration. Meanwhile, TBMs are used for larger diameter tunnels, employing a circular cutting head to bore through various rock types efficiently. Both types of equipment are crucial in construction and mining, optimizing excavation processes and enhancing project timelines.</p></p>
