<p><p>The teeth whitening kits market features four primary types: gels, pens, strips, and trays. Gel kits typically contain hydrogen or carbamide peroxide and are applied using custom or standard trays for effective whitening. Pens offer portability and convenience, delivering whitening gel directly onto teeth. Strips are thin, flexible plastic coated with whitening gel, designed for easy application. Trays provide a more comprehensive whitening approach, often moldable for a customized fit, ensuring even distribution of the whitening agent for optimal results.</p></p>
