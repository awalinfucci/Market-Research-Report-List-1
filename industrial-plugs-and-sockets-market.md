<p><p>The industrial plugs and sockets market is categorized into three main types: plugs, sockets, and mechanical interlocks. Plugs are electrical connectors designed to connect equipment to a power source, while sockets provide the receptacles for these plugs, enabling safe and efficient electrical connections. Mechanical interlocks enhance safety by preventing the disconnection of plugs under load, ensuring that machinery cannot be accidentally deactivated while still connected. Together, these components are essential for providing reliable, secure electrical interfaces in industrial environments.</p></p>
