<p><p>Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) systems are advanced imaging techniques used primarily in medical diagnostics. The market is segmented into two main types: Balloon Optical Probes and Balloon-less Optical Probes. Balloon Optical Probes utilize a balloon-assisted mechanism to enhance imaging in narrow or complex anatomical structures, improving the quality of diagnostic images. In contrast, Balloon-less Optical Probes offer a more streamlined approach, facilitating easier handling and access while maintaining high-resolution imaging capabilities for various medical applications.</p></p>
