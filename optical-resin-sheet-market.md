<p><strong>The Optical Resin Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Material</li><li>Thermoplastic Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Resin Sheet market is primarily divided into two types: thermosetting materials and thermoplastic materials. Thermosetting materials, once cured, cannot be remolded, offering superior thermal stability and mechanical strength, making them ideal for applications requiring durability. In contrast, thermoplastic materials are versatile and can be reshaped upon heating, providing ease of processing and recycling. Each type serves distinct applications in industries such as electronics, automotive, and optics, catering to varying performance and manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
