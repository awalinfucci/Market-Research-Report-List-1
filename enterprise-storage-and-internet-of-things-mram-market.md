<p><p>Enterprise Storage and Internet of Things (IoT) MRAM (Magnetoresistive RAM) market comprises two primary types: Toggle MRAM and Spin-Transfer Torque MRAM (STT-MRAM). Toggle MRAM utilizes magnetic switching to store data and is known for its fast access speeds and non-volatility, making it suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, STT-MRAM leverages spin-polarized currents to write data, allowing for higher density and lower power consumption. Both types are essential for enhancing the performance and efficiency of modern storage solutions in enterprise and IoT environments.</p></p>
