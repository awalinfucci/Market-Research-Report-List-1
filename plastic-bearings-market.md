<p><strong>The Plastic Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plastic Rolling Bearings</li><li>Plastic Sliding Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic bearings market is primarily divided into two types: plastic rolling bearings and plastic sliding bearings. </p><p>Plastic rolling bearings utilize balls or cylindrical rollers made from polymers, offering low friction and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for high-speed applications. In contrast, plastic sliding bearings operate on a sliding surface and often provide better load distribution and versatility in harsh environments. Both types are favored in industries like automotive, electronics, and machinery for their lightweight, durable, and maintenance-free characteristics.</p></p>
